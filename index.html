Building custom bags since 82'

Professional Bags for Professional People

Our founder, Alice Avis, is a 3rd generation seamstress. In 1982, she started Watts Baggs as a way to provide for her growing family. Alice's talents, creativity, and ability to design custom bags made her products superior to what was available in the market. Alice had a vision of building a company that could provide not only for her family but others in the community. She always tried to employ mothers that needed help to support their own families. Today, Watts Baggs is still a privately owned family business that follows Alice's original vision.

From the beginning, Watts Baggs has built high quality products to our customers' specifications and needs. Our ability to build custom bags has made us unique from other bag companies. This is why we can work with Fortune 500 companies down to small businesses, including work with international businesses, professional sports teams, and universities.
