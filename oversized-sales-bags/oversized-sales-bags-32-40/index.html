Oversized Sales Bags 32-40

Our oversized bags were designed for clients trying to hold all of their products/supplies in one bag. These heavy-duty luggage bags were designed and improved over the last two decades by active medical representatives. This evolutionary process has created a custom made bag built for everyday use with a wheel system that glides smoothly and quietly over all surfaces and compartments specifically customized to organize your unique supplies.

Not all vehicles can easily accommodate some of these bag sizes. Please measure your vehicle to ensure your bag selection will fit.

CUSTOM BAG FEATURES:

  • Embroidery options for up to 3 sides of the bag
  • Interior foam padding and exterior hard plastic to protect your equipment
  • Commercial grade materials
  • 8 zipper pockets for literature and product storage
  • Customizable interior with removable/adjustable divider system
  • Lid pouch pocket to carry long boxes outside of bag
  • Add-on strap that wraps around the handle of a briefcase, laptop bag, purse, etc
