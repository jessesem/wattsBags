Combo Sales Bags

Design your own bag set by choosing a heavy-duty rolling bag to hold your products like Brady/Tachy and a piggyback bag to hold your longer devices like catheters. You can attach a piggyback bag to the top of the rolling bag or use them separately. Our luggage sets attach together using our 3-buckle system: the top buckle supports the weight of the piggyback bag and two additional buckles secure the bag on each side. All piggyback bags have a padded shoulder strap and grab handle in order to use the bag separate from the rolling bag.

CUSTOM BAG FEATURES:

  • Ability to clip your bag sets together so you can roll them together as one
  • Embroidery options for up to 3 sides on the rolling bag and 2 sides on the piggyback bag
  • Foam padding throughout the inside and hard plastic on the outside to protect your equipment
  • Interior foam padding and exterior hard plastic to protect your equipment
  • Commercial grade materials
  • 8 zipper pockets for literature and product storage
  • Our Watts Bags exclusive wheel system
  • Customizable interior with removable/adjustable divider system
  • Ability to carry long boxes on the outside of the rolling bag
  • Add-on strap that wraps around the handle of a briefcase, laptop bag, purse, etc
